**Childhood and Training**

**1. Name:** John Wick's birth name is Jardani Jovonovich, from Belarus. 
**2. Orphaned:**  He was orphaned at a young age. 
**3. Ruska Roma:**  He was adopted by the Ruska Roma, a powerful criminal faction and syndicate, and raised by the Director of the Ruska Roma. 
**4. Assassin Training:**  He was trained at the Institute of Performing Arts in New York City, which served as a front for the Ruska Roma's assassin academy. 
**5. Skills:** 
- He is a master of hand-to-hand combat (a blend of martial arts like Judo and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu)
- proficiency with melee weapons and improvisation, stealth, driving and vehicular combat
- high degree of physical conditioning (strength, speed, agility, and endurance) and intellectual cunning
