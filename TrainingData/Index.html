John Wick (2014)

When arrogant mobsters steal his prized car and kill the puppy gifted to him by his late wife, legendary hitman John Wick is forced out of retirement. He unleashes a merciless, mythical-level rampage through the New York underworld he once dominated, reminding everyone why he is the one you send to kill the Boogeyman.

John Wick: Chapter 2 (2017)

Bound by a blood oath to a former associate, John Wick travels to Rome to honor his past. There, he is double-crossed and must fight his way through the world's deadliest assassins. The conflict escalates, putting him in the crosshairs of the High Table and culminating in a shocking act on consecrated ground, making him "excommunicado."
